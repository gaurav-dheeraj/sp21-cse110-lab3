7 April

Heard Rapture by Anita Baker - Spoiler: It's too good.

Anita Baker's R&B classic Rapture, dude what an album. The synths, the keys, the bass, the vocals. This is seriously good. I'd rate this a 54 outta 10 (wait, what?). Although the lyrics could get quite trite, the songwriting feels super fresh when compared to other 80's R&B classics. This veers away from the electro-R&B wave that dominated the 80's for something involved a cohesive mix of disco, post-disco, funk, fusion, soul, gospel and smooth jazz.
